Stellar cartography, also known as celestial cartography, uranography, or astrography, is the practice of mapping stars, galaxies, and other celestial objects on the celestial sphere. It's essentially the mapmaking of the cosmos! Throughout history, humans have looked to the stars for navigation,timekeeping, and storytelling. Early stellar cartography was based on observations. Modern techniques to create maps include:

  • Astrometry
  • Photometry
  • Spectroscopy
